About Me

Born in Brooklyn, Sarah has spent her life writing, singing, and dancing her way through New York City. She is now a first year student at Barnard College and works as a tutor. Sarah loves to traverse the lines between poetry and prose. She has received a Scholastic Gold Medal and several Scholastic Gold and Silver Keys, as well as an honorable mention in the City College of New York Poetry Contest for her poetry. She is an alumna of the Kenyon Review Young Writers Workshop as well as the Sewanee Young Writers' Conference. For more information on Sarah's professional and literary life, check out her LinkedIn.
